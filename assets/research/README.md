# Research Assets

User interview notes, affinity maps, screener PDFs, usability test summaries, and other research artifacts from my case studies.

## Organization

Drop research artifacts into subfolders named by project — one folder per case study. Suggested structure:

```
assets/research/
  cartlog/
    interview-notes-01.md
    interview-notes-02.md
    affinity-map.png
    screener-questions.md
  topper/
  aventi/
```

## What to include

- Interview transcripts or notes (anonymized — no participant PII)
- Screener questions used to recruit participants
- Affinity mapping images or exports
- Usability test session summaries
- Survey results (aggregated, not raw)
- Research plans and discussion guides

## What NOT to include

- Participant PII (real names, emails, phone numbers, screen recordings of identifiable faces)
- Confidential client research (any project done under NDA)
- Raw data that could re-identify a participant

If in doubt, anonymize. Every participant gets a pseudonym or an ID.
